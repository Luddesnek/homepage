My anime scoring system

(Disclaimer: this is "system" is subjective)

The way I've been scoring anime has been bugging me lately. Why? Well, it seems like it's starting to become complicated, and I'm not using any clear metrics when I score these shows, and I'd prefer having a structure so I could clearly remember how these shows are compared to each other and in general. I'm not thinking of making this system objective; The idea is to concretize subjectivity so I don't lose track of my opinions.

I kind of need this because I'd rather score something using an abstraction than a plain 1 to 10 metric, so I'll probably write reviews as well so I don't forget the shows. One question may be why I'm scoring shows, and I don't clearly know myself, but I guess I just like making value judgments, but at the same time, I don't like simple metrics.

Here's the idea:

 
10
 
- A show i will cherish for the rest of my life
 
9
 
- A truly great show that inspires and change me for the better
